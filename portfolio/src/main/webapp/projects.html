My Projects

  • Culturemesh: I worked on Culturemesh through Code the Change, a club that pairs student developers with nonprofit organizations. The Culturemesh website aims to connect the world's diaspora and help immigrants find their communities in new places.

  • Encode Usability Study: ENCODE, or the encyclopedia of DNA elements, is a massive database of genetic information, experiments, and sequences. This website was made for the purpose of a usability study to test the effectiveness of the current search engine on the ENCODE website, and suggest improvements.

  • Workforce (website down):
    • My project for Google's Computer Science Summer Institute in 2018. It uses Google Cloud Platform's natural language processing to provide feedback on resumès and help users find jobs.
