About Me

My name is Nathaniel Chien, a sophomore at Stanford studying computer science with a concentration in biocomputation. I was born and raised in the California central valley, but both my parents are immigrants; my mom emigrated from the Philippines and my dad from Hong Kong.

I'm interested in the intersection between computer science and biology, and exploring the applications that tech can have in research and healthcare. What draws me to tech is the potential to impact people across the world.
